About Anweshan

Welcome to Anweshan — where imagination meets innovation.

Here, science isn’t just about formulas and facts — it’s about turning your imagination into reality. We give you a platform to express your creativity, whether it’s through Rangoli making, poster design, or exhibiting your science projects.

Whether you’re building, experimenting, or dreaming up something entirely new, this is your stage to shine. Sign up for Anweshan 2025 today — it’s never too late for science!

Rules & Guidelines

1. Each group must have 4 participants. Projects should be science-based working or non-working models.
2. During the exhibition, 2 students will explain for 2 hours while the other 2 take a break. They will alternate roles to ensure active participation.
3. Participants must have complete knowledge of their exhibit. Only well-prepared models will be considered.
4. Elimination Round: After Diwali Vacation (3rd week of November).
5. Enrollment: Scroll down below to register by 7th November 2025.
